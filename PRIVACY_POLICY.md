# Privacy Policy — ICMP Ping (`com.mh.icmpclient`)

**Effective date:** March 22, 2026

## Summary

ICMP Ping is a network diagnostic tool. It sends ICMP echo requests (“pings”) to **hostnames or IP addresses that you choose** and shows results on your device. This policy describes what happens on your device and what we do with information.

## Data we collect

We **do not** operate user accounts for this app and **do not** ask you to sign in.

**Information stored on your device**

- **Ping configuration and results** you generate while using the app (for example: target host, timestamps, round-trip times, session summaries). This is stored **locally** on your device using on-device storage (Android Room database).
- **App preferences** you set in the app (for example: ping options), stored locally.

**Network activity**

- The app uses the **Internet** and **network state** permissions so it can send pings and, when you use background ping, run a **foreground service** with a visible notification. Network traffic goes between **your device** and the **hosts you ping** (or your network as required for that operation). **We do not receive copies of your ping traffic on our servers** because this app does not send your ping history or host list to us.

**Notifications**

- If you enable it, the app may show **notifications** related to ongoing ping activity (foreground service).

## Data sharing

We **do not sell** your personal information. We **do not** share ping history or session data with advertisers. Data you create stays on your device unless **you** export or share it (for example, if you use Android share or copy features elsewhere).

## Children

The app is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

You should protect access to your device. Anyone with access to your unlocked device could see data stored by the app.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The **Effective date** at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

You can contact the developer using the **support email** shown on this app’s Google Play store listing.

---

*This policy applies to the Android app package **com.mh.icmpclient** distributed on Google Play.*
